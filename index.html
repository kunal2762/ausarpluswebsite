    Meet Our Founder

    Rooted in Nature,
    Driven by Purpose

    "Ayurveda is not just a practice — it is a way of life. At Ausar Plus, every product we create carries the wisdom of generations, crafted to bring balance, healing, and harmony to your everyday life."

    Kamlesh Bagde founded Ausar Plus Ayurveda with a single belief — that nature holds the answer to modern wellness challenges. Drawing from the ancient science of Ayurveda, he built a brand that blends time-honored herbal traditions with the needs of today's health-conscious generation. Under his leadership, Ausar Plus has grown from a local vision into a trusted name across Maharashtra, reaching thousands of families with pure, effective, and sustainable Ayurvedic products.
